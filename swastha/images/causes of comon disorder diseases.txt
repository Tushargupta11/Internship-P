causes of common disorders::
1.Regular washing of clothes and household stuff like bed sheets,towel,pillow 
   covers,clothes.
2.Cleaning of utensils after usage, cleaning of machines after their usage.
3.Regular cleaning and dusting of house and workplace.
4.Consuming infected food and drinks.
5.Using contaminated cooking cutlery and utensils.
6.Ignoring illness/ symptoms of diseases at early stage and without treating 
   them allowing them to grow more and bigger.
7.Getting in contact with persons who are ill or havings symptons related to illness.
